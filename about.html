About Studio Pathy

Pathy was raised between artist and antique dealers , that's where her passion for art and design started. She increased this interest trough a job as an interior designer.

Pathy has a long-time passion for the visual craftsmanship of interior design as well as architecture and the creative process has always been a critical part of Pathy's life.

Trough her art, Pathy explores subtle patterns that makes each artwork unique and individual.

The aim is to create unique pieces that blend well with unqiue interirors
